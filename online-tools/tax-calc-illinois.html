We built this free crypto tax calculator for residents of Illinois who want to check calculations quickly. Our calculator is for informational purposes only. Please consult a CPA or tax professional. Use cryptocurrency tax accounting software if you have lots of trades.

Illinois has a flat tax rate of 4.95% that applies to both short-term and long-term capital gains. To calculate Federal taxes, please use our USA tax calculator.

How to Use the Tax Calculator

  1. Enter the Total Cost of First Purchase: This is the initial amount you spent to buy the cryptocurrency.
  2. Enter the Amount of Sale: This is the total amount you received when you sold the cryptocurrency.
  3. Click on the Calculate Tax button to get the estimated tax on your capital gains.

Note: This is an estimation and may not reflect your actual tax liability. Always consult with a tax professional when filing your taxes.
