What is FBTC?

FBTC (Fidelity Wise Origin Bitcoin Fund) is a spot Bitcoin ETF that allows investors to gain exposure to Bitcoin through traditional brokerage accounts. Each FBTC share represents fractional ownership of actual Bitcoin held by the fund.

Why Use This Calculator?

This calculator helps you:

  • Convert your FBTC holdings to their equivalent Bitcoin amount
  • Understand your actual Bitcoin exposure through FBTC shares
  • Compare FBTC holdings with direct Bitcoin holdings
