How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select Your Filing Status: Choose the option that corresponds to your tax filing status.
  2. Enter Your Total Income: Input your overall annual taxable income for 2025.
  3. Provide Cost Basis & Sale Amount: Enter what you paid and what you sold for.
  4. Select Holding Period: Short-term (≤1 year) is taxed as ordinary income. Long-term (>1 year) gets preferential rates.
  5. Calculate: Click the button to see your estimated federal tax.

Always consult with a tax professional for precise numbers. This calculator provides estimates based on 2025 IRS rates.
