What is MSTR?

MSTR (MicroStrategy) is a publicly traded company that holds a significant amount of Bitcoin on its balance sheet. By owning MSTR shares, investors gain indirect exposure to Bitcoin through the company's Bitcoin holdings.

Note on Share Count: While MicroStrategy has 245,459,000 regular outstanding shares, this calculator uses the fully diluted share count of 281,418,000. The diluted count includes potential additional shares from convertible securities and stock options, providing a more conservative estimate of Bitcoin exposure per share.
