Cryptocurrency Taxation in Mississippi

Key Points

  • Simple Rate Structure: Most taxpayers will pay 5% on their crypto gains.
  • Income-Based Taxation: Gains are treated as regular income.
  • No Distinction: Mississippi makes no distinction between short-term and long-term gains.
  • Mining Income: Crypto mining is treated as self-employment income and taxed at regular income rates.

Common Tax Situations

Trading & Investment

All crypto trades, including crypto-to-crypto transactions, are taxable events. Most traders will pay the 5% rate on their gains.

Tax Planning Tips

While Mississippi's tax system is relatively simple, keep detailed records of all transactions for accurate reporting. Consider timing sales to offset gains with losses.
