How to Use This Calculator

  1. Select Your Filing Status: Choose the option that corresponds to your tax filing status.
  2. Enter Your Total Income: Input your overall annual taxable income for 2025.
  3. Provide Cost Basis & Sale Amount: Enter what you paid and what you sold for.
  4. Select Holding Period: Short-term (less than 1 year) is taxed as ordinary income. Long-term (over 1 year) gets preferential rates.
  5. Select Your State: Choose your state to include estimated state income tax on your gains.
  6. View Results: The calculator auto-updates as you type, or click the button. You'll see federal tax, state tax, and total estimated tax.

Always consult with a tax professional for precise numbers. This calculator provides estimates based on 2025 IRS rates and uses marginal bracket calculations.
