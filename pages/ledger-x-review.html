üîß How to Use Ledger Nano X

Compatibility

While the Nano X uses its operating system on the device (named Bolos), it is still compatible with iOS, macOS, Android, Windows, and even Linux. It also works with desktop wallets like MyEtherWallet.

‚ö†Ô∏è BEWARE OF SCAMS

There have been scams involving fake versions of their hardware manager app, Ledger Live, on the Google Play Store. Make sure you only download the app through the link on the Ledger Live page on Ledger's site.

Step 1: Download Ledger Live

Download the official Ledger Live app from Ledger's website only.

Step 2: Select Your Device

Choose Nano X from the device selection menu.

Step 3: Initialize as New Device

Select "Initialize as a new device" for brand new devices.

Step 4: Set PIN Code

Create a secure PIN code on your device.

Step 5: Write Down Recovery Words

Write down the 24 recovery words that appear on your device screen.

Step 6: Pair with Device

Connect your Nano X to your phone via Bluetooth.

Step 7: Set App Password

Set a password for the Ledger Live app for additional privacy.

Step 8: Ready to Use!

Your device is now ready to use! The entire process takes about 5 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Trust a Ledger from a Reseller?

Ledger doesn't use anti-tampering seals on their packaging, as they can give a false sense of security. Instead, they have developed an online test called Root of Trust to verify authenticity.

Does Ledger Nano X work with iPhone?

Yes, Ledger Nano X connects to iPhones via Ledger's iOS app.

Has Ledger ever been hacked?

There has never been a known instance of a Ledger getting hacked in a non-laboratory setting. However, Ledger's e-commerce database was breached in 2020, but this did not affect customer funds.

Our e-commerce and marketing database leaked. We immediately fixed the breach. Contact and order details were involved. Your funds are safe.

- Pascal Gauthier, CEO, Ledger
