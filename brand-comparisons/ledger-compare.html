Compare Ledger Hardware Wallets

Ledger offers a range of hardware wallets to suit different needs and budgets. Use the selectors above to compare any two Ledger models side by side.

Ledger Model Lineup

  • Ledger Nano S Plus ($79): Entry-level model with USB-C, no Bluetooth, largest app storage in Nano line
  • Ledger Nano X ($149): Bluetooth-enabled for mobile, built-in battery, most popular model
  • Ledger Nano Gen5 ($199): New touchscreen model with 2.8" E Ink display, NFC, EAL6+ security, Clear Signing
  • Ledger Flex ($249): Large 2.8" E Ink touchscreen, modern credit-card form factor, EAL6+ certified
  • Ledger Stax ($399): Premium curved E Ink display, wireless charging, customizable lock screen, NFC

Key Differences to Consider

  • Display: Nano S Plus/X have small OLED screens with buttons; Gen5/Flex/Stax have large E Ink touchscreens
  • Security Certification: Nano S Plus/X are EAL5+; Gen5/Flex/Stax are EAL6+ certified
  • Mobile Support: All models except Nano S Plus have Bluetooth for iOS/Android
  • NFC: Gen5 and Stax include NFC; others do not
  • Clear Signing: Gen5/Flex/Stax support human-readable transaction signing
  • Form Factor: Nano line is USB stick style; Flex/Stax are card-shaped; Gen5 is compact touchscreen

All Ledger devices support 5,500+ cryptocurrencies, work with Ledger Live, and use secure element chips to protect your private keys.
